News

An International conference dedicated to the development and peaceful use of atomic energy was held in Samarkand

On December 5, 2024, an international conference dedicated to the development and peaceful use of atomic energy was held in Samarkand.

Atomic Energy as a Stable and Environmentally Friendly Source of Electricity

As part of the International Conference program, a plenary session was held focusing on the prospects of atomic energy usage in OIC countries, including the development of atomic energy in Uzbekistan.

Important strategic documents were signed in Samarkand

At the International Conference "Perspective use of nuclear energy for peaceful purposes in sustainable development of Organization of Islamic Cooperation member states: international and national experience", held on December 5, 2024, in Samarkand, important strategic documents were signed, contributing to the strengthening of international cooperation in the field of atomic energy.

A press briefing

At International Conference on atomic energy, a press briefing was held featuring the Director General of the IAEA, Rafael Mariano Grossi, and the Director of the Atomic Energy Agency under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Azim Akhmedkhadjev.

IAEA and Nuclear Safety in Uzbekistan

The role of international organizations, particularly the IAEA, in supporting the peaceful use of nuclear energy and ensuring the safety of nuclear facilities in accordance with international norms and requirements was discussed during one of the panel sessions.

Nuclear Energy for Sustainable Development

During the next session of the conference, titled "Environmental and Social Aspects of the Peaceful Use of Nuclear Energy in OIC Countries for Achieving Sustainable Development," experts focused on the socio-economic and environmental dimensions of nuclear energy usage, as well as the impact of these technologies on the environment and public health.

Nuclear Technologies as a Driver of Scientific and Technological Progress in OIC Countries

As part of the international conference, a session was held to discuss the prospects of adopting and developing nuclear technologies for peaceful purposes in the member states of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OIC). Participants highlighted the significant potential of nuclear technologies as a powerful tool for scientific, technological, and economic advancement.

Innovation and Education for the Development of Atomic Energy in OIC Countries

Speakers at the final panel session of the international conference discussed key priorities in education, innovation, and technology integration for the peaceful use of atomic energy in OIC countries.

Modern challenges in physics, energy and thermal engineering were discussed

Modern challenges in physics, energy and thermal engineering were also discussed during the international conference.

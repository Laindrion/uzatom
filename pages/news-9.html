Modern challenges in physics, energy and thermal engineering were discussed

Modern challenges in physics, energy and thermal engineering were also discussed during the international conference. The scientific dialogue among leading scientists and experts in this field was organized by the Tashkent branch of the National Research Nuclear University MEPhI. The keynote presentations covered topics such as:


  • Nuclear education and the integration of nuclear power plants into energy systems,
  • Mega-science infrastructure,
  • Nuclear medicine,
  • The use of AI and aerostats for nuclear power plant safety.

The conference served as a platform for knowledge exchange and dialogue between science and business. Participants expressed their readiness to continue collaboration to address global scientific and technological challenges.

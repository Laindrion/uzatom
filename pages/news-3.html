Important strategic documents were signed in Samarkand

At the International Conference "Perspective use of nuclear energy for peaceful purposes in sustainable development of Organization of Islamic Cooperation member states: international and national experience", held on December 5, 2024, in Samarkand, important strategic documents were signed, contributing to the strengthening of international cooperation in the field of atomic energy. Each of the documents represents an important step forward in establishing productive cooperation and sharing experience in the use of atomic energy. During the international conference, the following documents were signed:

  • Additions to the Framework Country Program between the Atomic Energy Agency under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan and the International Atomic Energy Agency;
  • Memorandum of Understanding between the Atomic Energy Agency under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan and World Nuclear Association;
  • Memorandum of Cooperation between Nuclear Power Plant Construction Directorate and China National Nuclear Corporation Overseas;
  • Memorandum of Cooperation between the Atomic Energy Agency under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan and the Institute of Nuclear Physics of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan;
  • Agreement between the Atomic Energy Agency under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan and ASSYSTEM ENGINEERING & OPERATION SERVICES;
  • Memorandum of Understanding and Cooperation between the Branch of the Federal State Autonomous Educational Institution of Higher Education "National Research Nuclear University MEPhI" in Tashkent and the Research Institute for the Development of Digital Technologies and Artificial Intelligence.

Media accreditation

Dear Representatives of the Mass Media,

In accordance with the legislative requirements of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the decision on the accreditation of temporary foreign correspondents may take up to two weeks. Consequently, as of November 21 of this year, registration for the Conference in the "Media" category will be closed.

Representatives of mass media, not accredited in Uzbekistan, must be accredited by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

The accreditation procedure for representatives of foreign mass media representatives in Uzbekistan is available here: https://mfa.uz/en/pages/accreditation

Contacts of the Media Department: mediaconference@uzatom.uz

IAEA and Nuclear Safety in Uzbekistan

The role of international organizations, particularly the IAEA, in supporting the peaceful use of nuclear energy and ensuring the safety of nuclear facilities in accordance with international norms and requirements was discussed during one of the panel sessions. The speakers focused on the core principles, requirements, and recommendations for nuclear safety based on IAEA safety standards, national legislative regulation of the nuclear sector under international treaties and standards, their implementation and adaptation in Uzbekistan, as well as ensuring legislative and institutional coordination with international regulatory bodies.

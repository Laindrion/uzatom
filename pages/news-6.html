Nuclear Energy for Sustainable Development

During the next session of the conference, titled "Environmental and Social Aspects of the Peaceful Use of Nuclear Energy in OIC Countries for Achieving Sustainable Development," experts focused on the socio-economic and environmental dimensions of nuclear energy usage, as well as the impact of these technologies on the environment and public health. Special attention was given to public perception of nuclear energy and strategic approaches to citizen engagement and awareness. Experts emphasized that transparency and open dialogue with society are essential components for the successful adoption of nuclear technologies. Participants highlighted the importance of nuclear technologies for sustainable development, particularly in the context of energy security, reducing carbon emissions, and ensuring environmental stability.

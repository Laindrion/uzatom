A press briefing

At International Conference on atomic energy, a press briefing was held featuring the Director General of the IAEA, Rafael Mariano Grossi, and the Director of the Atomic Energy Agency under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Azim Akhmedkhadjev. In his speech, Rafael Mariano Grossi emphasized the significance of international cooperation among OIC countries in the field of atomic energy, highlighting its potential for sustainable development, economic stability, and combating climate change. He stressed the importance of transferring international practices and developing educational programs that ensure the safe use of atomic energy. Azim Akhmedkhadjayev, in turn, expressed gratitude for the results of the conference, which was attended by 500 delegates, including 150 representatives from 30 countries. He noted the key importance of engaging international partners in the project for building small modular nuclear power plants in Uzbekistan. Akhmedkhadjaev emphasized that international cooperation plays a critical role in ensuring energy security and addressing climate change, expressing hope for continued dialogue among participating countries. The conference served as an effective platform for exchanging opinions and knowledge among experts and young professionals in the field of atomic energy.
